General FAQ

How do I know my online order went through?

An SMS notification will be sent to your phone number as an order confirmation. Your order's basic details, such as the price, the content, etc should also be available in the message. If you have signed up for membership, and email will be sent to you as well.

What benefits do I get from the membership?

As you spend money at LetYouEatMyNoodle, you will collect points equivalent to the amount of money you spend. Once enough points have been collected, you can redeem for gifts such as coupons, company merch, or even a free bowl of your choice! What's more, signing up for membership is completely free! Just make sure to spend the points before they disappear after two months!

How do I refund or edit an order after placing it?

Please call our LetYouEatMyNoodle Customer Service Number at +601-0240-7930 to edit or cancel your order. At minimum, your call should be made within 10 minutes after placing your order.

About our Food

What makes the noodles of LetYouEatMyNoodle unique?

Our recipes are from the soulful homecooking of a thoroughly traditional Kedah household, the Tan household. It takes the beloved Kedahian comfort food and gives it a homely spin, transforming it into an internationally unique delight to the tongue!

Are all the ingredients supplied fresh?

As a food chain that prides itself in its freshness, all ingredients are supplied, prepped, cooked, at served during the same day. Our restaurants have a 'No-Frozen' policy on which our refridgerators are cleared out every two days, ensuring that our food is always as fresh as can be.
